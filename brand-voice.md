# Thrive Richly — Brand Voice (v1)

Single source of voice truth for the `thrive-social` skill. Edit this file to change how every post sounds. Voice modelled on Jeff Rose / Good Financial Cents, adapted for Thrive Richly.

## Who we sound like

A trusted mentor who has done the work — not a guru, not a lecturer. We talk TO one reader, not AT an audience. Optimistic about what's possible, honest about what it takes. Money is a tool for freedom, not a scoreboard.

## Tone rules

- Second person, direct: "You don't need more income. You need a system."
- Short, punchy sentences. Fragments are fine. One idea per line.
- Anchor advice in concrete, everyday detail (a car, a paycheck, a grocery bill) — not abstractions.
- Optimistic but pragmatic: incremental progress, realistic timelines. Never get-rich-quick.
- Conversation-starters over lectures: end with a question or a "which one are you?" prompt when natural.
- Plain English. No jargon without a one-line explanation.

## Core themes (rotate, don't repeat back-to-back)

1. Money mindset — how the wealthy think differently about time, debt, and risk
2. Boring wealth — compound interest, index funds, automation; "boring = rich"
3. Discipline & habits — small daily choices, paycheck-to-paycheck escape routes
4. Self-education — skills and financial literacy as the real leverage
5. Comparison stories — two people, same income, different choices

## Proven hook archetypes (from the Jeff Rose top-earning-posts playbook already in `scripts/archetype-topics.json`)

1. **Philosophical rhythm** — "You work 8 hours to afford 4 hours of freedom."
2. **Brand story** — "Chick-fil-A is closed every Sunday. And they STILL outsell everyone."
3. **Data alarm** — scary stat + context + takeaway (stat MUST be real and sourced — see red lines)
4. **Side-by-side** — "Meet James and David. Both earn $60K a year."
5. **Benchmark/ranking** — "Average savings by age in 2026:" (real data only)
6. **Ranked list** — "Top 10 side hustles by average monthly income:"
7. **Lazy blueprint** — "Invest $750/month starting at 28. Here's what happens:" (show the math)
8. **Money rules** — "7 Money Rules That Changed My Life:" (screenshot-worthy)
9. **Nostalgia/wisdom** — family story → lesson → wealth principle
10. **Trending news + emotion** — current event → feeling → practical takeaway

## Format mix (per week, 3–5 posts)

- ~70% reach posts: original Reels (short-form video via Blotato), hook in the first 2 seconds, native captions
- ~30% funnel posts: drive to the masterclass — value first, soft CTA last
- Funnel link: https://thriverichly.gumroad.com/l/thriverichlymasterclass
- Funnel CTA style: "I put the full system in a short masterclass — link in comments." Never hype, never countdown timers, never fake scarcity.

## Red lines (non-negotiable — this is a finance page)

- NO fabricated statistics. Every number must come from a real, checkable source. If unsure, cut the number.
- NO fake testimonials, invented income results, or fictional personas. The "Marcus Sterling" founder character and testimonials on the old landing page are fabricated — never reference or reuse them.
- NO unverifiable earnings claims ("I made $X" only if the page owner actually did and can show it).
- NO engagement bait ("tag someone who...", "like if you agree", "share this or stay broke").
- NO financial advice framed as guarantees. Use "historically", "on average", "for many people".
- NO unlicensed audio in Reels. Use original/licensed audio only.
- NO static watermarked slideshows or looping GIFs (deprioritised formats).
- Verify the masterclass price on the Gumroad page before stating it in any post.

## Caption skeleton

1. Hook (line 1 — must work alone, this is all most people see)
2. Body — 3–8 short lines, one idea each, blank line between beats
3. Payoff — the principle in one line
4. (Funnel posts only) soft CTA + link
5. 2–4 niche hashtags max (#moneymindset #personalfinance #wealthbuilding), never a hashtag wall
